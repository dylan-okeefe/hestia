# Discord Voice Onboarding — Husband Guide

**Goal:** Get set up to talk to Hestia hands-free on your AirPods (or any Bluetooth headset) while you do chores.

**What this is:** Hestia lives in a Discord voice channel called `#hestia-voice`. You join the channel, talk naturally, and she responds out loud. No phone-screen interaction needed.

**What you need:**
- A Discord account (free)
- The Discord mobile app (iOS or Android)
- AirPods or any Bluetooth headset/earbuds

---

## Step 1: Create a Discord account

1. Download the **Discord** app from the App Store or Google Play.
2. Open the app and tap **Register**.
3. Enter your email, choose a username, set a password, and enter your birthdate.
4. Verify your email (Discord will send a link — tap it).

## Step 2: Accept the server invite

Dylan will send you an invite link (looks like `https://discord.gg/xxxxxx`).

1. Tap the link.
2. Discord opens and shows the server name.
3. Tap **Accept Invite**.

You are now in the server. You should see a text channel (`#general` or similar) and a voice channel (`#hestia-voice`).

## Step 3: Connect your AirPods (or headset)

**AirPods:**
1. Put them in your ears.
2. Open the AirPods case near your phone.
3. Follow the on-screen prompt to connect.

**Other Bluetooth headset:**
1. Put the headset in pairing mode (usually hold the power button until it blinks).
2. On your phone: **Settings → Bluetooth → tap the headset name**.

## Step 4: Join the voice channel

1. In Discord, tap the server icon (left sidebar).
2. Under **Voice Channels**, tap **`#hestia-voice`**.
3. You will see a panel at the bottom saying "Voice Connected."

## Step 5: Talk to Hestia

Just speak naturally. Examples:

- "What's on my calendar today?"
- "Add milk to the shopping list."
- "Remind me to take out the trash at 7 PM."

Hestia will respond out loud through your AirPods.

### Tips for best results

- **Pause briefly** between sentences so Hestia knows you're done speaking.
- **Speak clearly** — she understands normal conversation but struggles with heavy background noise.
- **One person at a time** — if Dylan is also in the channel, avoid talking over each other.
- **"Over" keyword** (optional): if Hestia keeps cutting you off mid-thought, say "over" at the end of your sentence. This forces her to wait for your full thought. Example: "Check the weather for tomorrow, over."

## Step 6: Leave the channel

Tap the **Disconnect** button (phone icon with an X) at the bottom of the screen.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Hestia doesn't respond | Check that you're in `#hestia-voice` (not a text channel). Make sure your mic isn't muted in Discord. |
| I can't hear her | Check your AirPods/headset volume. Make sure Discord has permission to use Bluetooth audio. |
| She responds to Dylan, not me | This is normal if you talk at the same time. Wait for Dylan's exchange to finish, then speak. |
| She misunderstood me | Say "cancel that" or "never mind" and rephrase. |
| Background noise triggers her | Move to a quieter spot, or mute your mic when not speaking. |

## Privacy note

- Only Dylan, you, and Hestia are in this private server.
- Hestia does not record raw audio. She transcribes what you say, processes it, and discards the audio.
- Transcripts are stored locally on Dylan's computer, not on Discord's servers.

## Need help?

Ask Dylan — he can check the logs, adjust volume, or restart Hestia if something goes wrong.
